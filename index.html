Who we are

The Association of Petroleum Distributers is a federation representing the petroleum companies that energize our economy. We represent 26 distributers across the United states. We work to advocate for these corporations in the law, to better help them serve you.

Our advocacy

Our primary issues surround transportation and point of sale of petroleum products in the United States. We write requests, and work with regulators to produce a solution that allows our members to thrive.

Our goals

We are perpetually pursuing proposals populated by our petroleum partners. Here are a few examples:

- New underground storage tank regulation is stifling innovation. We are pressuring regulators to rollback or delay this policy. The current direction of this case is advocating for a 3 year extention to allow for safe and thoughtful implemention of the new requirements.
